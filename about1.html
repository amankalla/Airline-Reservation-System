Air India Services

With Ground Handling setup at over 50 airports in India, Air India Airport Services is a One Stop Shop for the comprehensive range of GH services including Security, Flight Operations & Technical Handling services which will be provided through its parent company. Air India Airport Services with the experience and expertise, coupled with a young and vibrant workforce is willing to take on any challenge.

Besides Narrow Body aircraft's, which Air India Airport Services handles at most of the airports in India, it has the capability to handle wide body aircraft's at Mumbai (BOM), Chennai (MAA), Kolkata (CCU), Kochi (COK), Kozhikode (CCJ), Ahmedabad (AMD), Amritsar (ATQ), Lucknow (LKO), Guwahati (GAU) and Goa (GOI).

From being the first and only Ground Handler in India to handle the Airbus A380 on its maiden visit to India in 2007, to handling the futuristic 787 Dreamliners at major airports in India, Air India Airport Services is the Ground Handler for all your needs.
